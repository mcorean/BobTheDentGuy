WHO IS BOB?

Bob has been specialized in fixing car dents and scratches. He started 20 years ago with a loan from his father to buy the tools necessary for the job. 20 years later he is still serving the tri-state area with great service and great results.

Bob offers free estimates, and you can even text him a picture of your car to get an estimate without having to leave your house. No need to spend time driving to a body shop only to get an expensive bill and have to either wait there or get a ride home and come back the next day. Give Bob a call if you have any questions about any dents, scratches, or dings that you are tired of looking at. Because they don't have to be there forever!
